Health Data Notice

Consumer Health Privacy

This notice applies to consumer-facing data collected in connection with demonstrations, service support, and digital interactions involving health-related contexts.

Last reviewed: March 15, 2026

Scope of This Notice

We may process health-context data points only to provide product support, quality improvements, and documented service requests. We do not sell personally identifiable health information.

Data Categories

  • Support request metadata and issue context provided by authorized users.
  • Device diagnostics required to troubleshoot product and service issues.
  • Communication records related to consented service interactions.

Your Controls

  • Request access or correction where legally permissible.
  • Request deletion of data that is no longer required for legal or service obligations.
  • Manage communication preferences and consent updates.
Submit privacy requests at contact@sahrangmedtech.systems with subject: Consumer Health Privacy Request.
