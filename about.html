About Sahrang Medtech

We build practical digital uroflowmetry systems for clinics and care teams that need reliable measurements, connected workflows, and affordability without compromising clinical confidence.

Established in India, serving hospitals, clinics, and tele-urology teams.

Our Mission

To modernize uroflowmetry with accessible medical technology that improves diagnostic quality and patient experience across urban and semi-urban care settings.

What we deliver

  • AI-assisted interpretation support for clinical decision workflows.
  • Cloud-ready records and secure report sharing across teams.
  • Deployment models designed for India-first healthcare operations.

Operating Principles

  1. Patient dignity and privacy come first in every workflow we design.
  2. Clinical usability matters as much as technical capability.
  3. Transparent documentation and post-sale support are non-negotiable.
  4. Continuous product improvement based on practitioner feedback.
