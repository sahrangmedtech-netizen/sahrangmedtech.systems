Culture

Why Sahrang

We are a small team that moves deliberately. We value depth over velocity and long-term thinking over short-term outcomes.

Meaningful Work

Every role contributes to a product used in real clinical environments. The feedback loop between your work and patient outcomes is direct and measurable.

Intellectual Rigour

We approach problems carefully. Decisions are grounded in data, domain expertise, and honest assessment of constraints — not assumptions.

Collaborative Ownership

Roles here are broad by design. You will work closely across engineering, clinical, and commercial functions, and take genuine ownership of outcomes.

Growth Environment

Early-stage companies create opportunities that do not exist elsewhere. You will develop skills and responsibilities at a pace that a larger organisation cannot offer.

High Standards

Medical-grade software demands precision. We have a culture of thorough review, clear documentation, and accountability to the clinicians who rely on us.

Sustainable Pace

We build for the long term. We do not celebrate burnout. Consistent, focused work over sustained periods produces better outcomes than short-lived intensity.

Process

How We Hire

We keep our process transparent and respectful of your time. Every step is designed so both sides can make a confident, well-informed decision.

01

Application

Submit your CV and a brief note on why the role interests you. We read every application personally and respond to all candidates.

02

Initial Review

We assess your background carefully against the role's requirements and respond within 5 business days with a clear, honest outcome.

03

Intro Call

A 30-minute conversation with the hiring manager to discuss the role, your background, and mutual expectations openly.

04

Assessment

A focused, role-relevant technical evaluation — either a short take-home task or a live working session with the immediate team.

05

Final Round

A final conversation with senior leadership to align on values, long-term expectations, and offer terms before a decision is extended.
